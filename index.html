Help Center

Recording & Uploading Your Audio

  • Format: All audio files are accepted (MP3, WAV, OGG, AAC, M4A, FLAC, WMA, OPUS, AIFF, AMR, MIDI, etc.).
  • Size: Must be ≤ 150MB. Use a lower bitrate if needed.
  • Content: Clearly state your name, and any relevant details.

Filling Out the Form

  • Full Name: Letters, spaces, hyphens, apostrophes only – no numbers.
  • Phone Number: 9 to 14 digits (you can start with +).
  • Index Number: Must begin with NTCDGRGN240 followed by exactly 3 digits from 001 to 190. Example: NTCDGRGN240001 (valid), NTCDGRGN240190 (valid), NTCDGRGN240000 (invalid), NTCDGRGN240250 (invalid).
  • Tech Support Number: Click "Save" to add O.G's number to your device contacts (required).
💡 Each index number can be used only once. Duplicate submissions are blocked.
