Help Center

Preparing Your Documents

  • Hard copies → Soft copies: Go ang get your documents professionally scanned by a scanner at a printing shop. Do not use a smartphone scanning app (e.g., Adobe Scan, CamScanner)!
  • File format: Save as PDF for all certificates and IDs. For the passport photo, use JPG or PNG.
  • File size: Each file must be ≤ 5MB. Reduce size by using lower resolution if needed.
  • Naming: Use descriptive file names (e.g., "John_Doe_Certificate.pdf") to avoid confusion.

Filling Out the Form

  • Full Name: Enter your legal name exactly as on your ID.
  • Phone / WhatsApp: Use your active whatsapp line. I'll use WhatsApp for updates and contacting.
  • Passport Photo: Recent, clear, and facing forward.
  • High School Certificate: Scanned copy of your certificate or result slip.
  • Birth Certificate: Official birth record.
  • Testimonial: A reference letter from a previous employer or institution.
  • Ghana Card: Both front and back sides, clearly visible. Note that the front and back should all be separated into different pdf files.
💡 Tip: After successful payment and submission, you can edit any field to resubmit if needed. All documents are stored securely in the cloud.
